What is Amazon Review Miner?

Amazon Review Minor is a program that helps buyers understand what the users of a product generally feel about their experience after purchase. By using A.I machine learning technology, the code scans through every review of a product. By analyzing diction of reviews, the program translates qualitative user experiences into quantitative data easily understood by anyone.

How is this project used?

This project is currently being used to help potential customers easily navigate through Amazon when determining whether or not to purchase a product. Open to anyone, it’s purpose is to make buying an easier, effective, and enjoyable experience. In the future, I plan to use this machine learning A.I to expand its purposes beyond simply Amazon--tackling other commonly used online shops. By extension of growing our use to more websites, the project can generate valuable data for research projects related public perception in the market on various products.

Who is this project for?

This project is open for anyone making a purchase on Amazon. For especially those who aren’t committed to buying a product, this code will help identify how others generally felt with their experience. Products with hundreds to even thousands of reviews can be often confusing to navigate in order to properly understand what it offers unless you spend hours reading reviews. For people in these situations, this solution easily crunches the information into numbers and fewer words that could be recognized instantly. In order to use, simply insert the URL of the Amazon page and press start in order for it to operate. It’s as easy as that.
